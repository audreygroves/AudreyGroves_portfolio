EcoDrive Connect

Audrey collaborated with a team of junior designers and product managers, contributing to the full spectrum of product creation processes, from user research and user story creation to journey mapping and prototyping.

The solution, EcoDrive Connect, stands out as a dual-sided digital engagement tool. It enables auto financers to leverage tax incentives and other sustainable finance commitments, allowing them to offer preferred loan rates to drivers who actively offset their emissions. The goal is to distinguish auto financers from the competition, particularly among younger, environmentally conscious customers.

This comprehensive solution secured a victory among 20+ international teams at the IdeaLab product innovation competition.
